Privacy Policy

The "Work Shift Calendar" app (hereinafter — "the App") is designed for individual use. We value our users' trust and prioritize the protection of your personal information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed so that the Developer has no direct access to your personal records. All data regarding your work shifts, rates, advances, and other earnings is stored exclusively:

  • Locally: On your device using Apple's SwiftData technology.
  • In the Cloud: In your personal encrypted iCloud (CloudKit) container for synchronization between your devices.

2. Technical Support

When you contact support via email, the App generates a message containing technical information about your device. This is used solely for error diagnostics:

  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 15)
  • Operating system version (iOS/iPadOS)
  • App version
  • Interface language settings

You have the right to delete this data from the email body before sending it.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Currently, the App is completely free and does not contain advertising modules or analytical systems that transfer data to third parties. We do not sell or exchange your personal information.

4. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this Policy. If features are introduced that change the way data is processed (e.g., ads or paid subscriptions), information will be promptly published on this page.
